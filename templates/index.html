Book summarization with NLP

This is a simple showcase of my exploration into automatic summarization of texts, using state-of-the-art NLP models. In particular, this is an attempt to summarize books, which must be the most difficult usecase for automatic summarization.

The books are sourced from Project Gutenberg and are therefore in the public domain.
